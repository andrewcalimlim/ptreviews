Who I Am

My name is Andrew Calimlim!

I am an undergraduate student at Columbia University studying computer science. Right now, I am on a year-long hiatus from Columbia, taking classes at the University of Washington as a part-time student.

My main interests in CS are in UX design and database management.

I've grown up in the greater Seattle area my entire life and one of my favorite foods for as long as I can remember is phad thai. I wanted to combine my pride for the city and my love of this classic staple of street food from Thailand to create an archive for my opinions on the food in the area.

I have never been to Thailand, however I would like to visit someday.
