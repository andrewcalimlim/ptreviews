Mali Thai and the Importance of Juxtaposition

July 25, 2018

Mali Thai resides in Tukwila next to a Red Robin and a store selling christian memorabilia. It's rather dark inside and half of the seating are these wooden, basket-like booths that you can't really enter or exit without hitting your knee on the edge of it. Though once you're seated and have adjusted to the dimness, the atmosphere becomes quite calm.

On my first visit, my parents and I ate family-style (ordering and sharing several dishes). The beef yellow curry with the chicken phad thai turned out to pair wonderfully. The phad thai was a great contrast to the already delicious curry--more than the rice that comes with it.

My perspective on the phad thai changed when I ordered it on its own on my second visit. The taste was good, but underwhelming. The taste of the bean sprouts felt stronger. The portions though, were enough to feed me before my plans that Saturday night and then at 3 AM while I attempted to recover from said plans.

Rating: 7/10
